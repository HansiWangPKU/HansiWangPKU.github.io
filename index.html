Timeline

  • 2026-07 Honored to receive a SAC Highlight at ACL 2026. A short academic journey from 2022 to 2026, but at least it left behind one small piece I can call my own.
  • 2026-04 Completed a small "grand slam" across the three classic NLP venues — ACL, EMNLP & COLING. Even if these venues are now half-jokingly called "story-telling conferences," and even though NAACL is arguably more deserving of a place among the "big three," while COLING is even often seen as ranking below Findings.
  • 2025-11 My paper was luckily accepted to the EMNLP main conference as an oral, with an OA3 rating. Honestly I couldn't agree with the 3/3/3 scores from my three reviewers — they just didn't get it.
  • 2024-11 My first first-author paper was accepted. Still felt lost — a long way from the frontier of research in the age of AI.
  • 2024-06 My first paper was rejected by CCL 2024. The reviewers weren't impressed. That paper — an improved version of Cilin — cost me enormous effort refining the data and handling all kinds of word-sense issues.
